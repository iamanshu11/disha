About Us

Your Local Experts, with Global Reach

Disha Communication is a full-service agency with over three decades of expertise, blending big agency thinking with a nimble, client-centered approach. With branches across India and access to over 30 international markets, we partner with brands looking to scale their impact. Our approach is simple – innovative ideas, executed meticulously to fit your budget and your timeline.

Nigel Mathew

A Partner in Your Brand’s Journey

At Disha Communication, I’m not just leading a team—I’m championing the stories, visions, and goals of our clients. Every brand has a unique journey, and my role is to be more than an advisor; it’s to be a true partner, walking with you at each step.

Whether collaborating with household names or inspiring start-ups, I believe in the power of creative work that connects and leaves a lasting impact. Disha’s expansion globally isn’t just about growing a business—it’s about building trusted relationships, where quality, understanding, and timely results come together to help our clients shine on the world stage.

If you’re ready to elevate your brand with a team that’s committed to you, I’d love to connect. Let’s create something exceptional together.
